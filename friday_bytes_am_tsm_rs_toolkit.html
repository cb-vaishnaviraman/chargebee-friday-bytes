Renewals & amendments best practices

🔄 Renewal opps are auto-created. Once a contract is Closed Won - Booked and Activated, a renewal opportunity auto-generates — find it under the contract's "Renewal Opportunity" section.
✏️ Amendments start on the contract page. Click "Amend Contract" to handle upsells, cross-sells, downsells, or product swaps. Co-terming keeps all subscriptions aligned to one renewal date.
📅 Start renewal conversations 90 days out. Don't wait for the auto-trigger — build the relationship before the clock starts ticking.
📊 Log everything. Every call, email & meeting against the Account and Opportunity record — your forecast accuracy depends on it.
⚠️ Never skip Contact Role. It's a mandatory field before any contract moves to Closed Won — missing it blocks the RSO validation.

Opportunity stages for AM / RS / TSM

📍 Value Alignment (20%) — SC auto-assigned here. Confirm account details & expansion scope.
📍 Solution Alignment (40%) — technical win in progress. Keep SC aligned.
📍 Scoping / Proposing (60%) — primary quote required to advance.
📍 Negotiating (80%) — approved quote required before Pre Close.
📍 Closed Won - Pending (99%) — SpotDraft contract signed + all mandatory fields filled.
📍 Closed Won - Booked (100%) — RSO validates → contract auto-created → activate it.

Renewal cadence best practices

📅 Start your renewal cadence 90 days out. Don't wait until 30 days — by then you're negotiating, not nurturing.
✉️ Personalise every touch. Reference the customer's usage, wins, and growth since they signed. Generic renewal emails get ignored.
📞 Multi-channel wins. Email + call + LinkedIn = 77–91% more responses vs email alone. Especially critical for at-risk accounts.
⚠️ All SalesLoft activity auto-syncs to SFDC only if the Account/Contact record exists. Log call outcomes manually — don't skip dispositions.

SpotDraft
Contract creation & e-signature for renewals and amendments. Every renewal or contract change must go through SpotDraft before it can be marked Closed Won. ✍️

Renewal & amendment contract workflow

1️⃣ Click "New Contract" on the Opportunity page → "Create contract from template" → select the right renewal or amendment template.
2️⃣ Fill in details → click Next → "Send for signature" → choose recipient.
3️⃣ Once signed — fill all mandatory SFDC fields → mark as Closed Won - Pending.
4️⃣ For amendments — click "Amend Contract" on the contract page. Choose the type: Upsell, Cross-sell, Downsell, or Remove Products → new Amendment Opportunity auto-creates.
⚠️ Contract must be in "Activated" status before amendments are available. Always activate after Closed Won - Booked.
