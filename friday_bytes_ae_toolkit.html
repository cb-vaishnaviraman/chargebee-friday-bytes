Cadence essentials

1️⃣ Enrol prospects — right cadence for their deal stage.
2️⃣ Personalise before sending — 20% personalisation = 2× reply rate.
3️⃣ Multi-channel it — email + call + LinkedIn = 77–91% more replies.
4️⃣ Hot lead? Drop everything — run Call Now cadence immediately.
5️⃣ Final "Other" step — decide: nurture, re-enrol, or remove.

Salesforce sync — auto vs manual

✅ Auto-syncs: emails sent, calls logged, cadence steps — as long as SFDC record exists.
✏️ Log manually: call outcomes/notes & meeting next steps. Log before you move on.
⚠️ Gaps that break sync: no SFDC record, personal email, skipped disposition, unfilled dynamic fields.

SpotDraft AE Only
Contract creation & e-signature tool. Before any deal is Closed Won — the contract must be created, signed & sent through SpotDraft. No signature = no booking. ✍️

How to use SpotDraft — step by step

1️⃣ Click "New Contract" on the Opportunity page in Salesforce.
2️⃣ Select "Create contract from template" → choose the right template for the deal type.
3️⃣ Fill in the details → click Next.
4️⃣ Click "Send for signature" → choose the recipient and send.
5️⃣ Once signed — fill all mandatory SFDC fields (Greenlane Status, RAG Deal Review, Primary Competitor, Chargebee Customer ID, Contact Role) → mark as Closed Won - Pending.
6️⃣ RSO validates → moves to Closed Won - Booked. Contract auto-creates in SFDC → activate by changing status to "Activated".

Chilipiper AE Only
Instant meeting routing & scheduling. AEs use it to accept handoff meetings from XDRs and manage disco call outcomes directly in Salesforce. ⚡

AE workflow in Chilipiper

📥 Handoff meeting booked by XDR via the "Handoff Meeting" button on the Opportunity page — auto-links to an Event record in SFDC.
✅ After the disco call, update "Meeting Status" on the Event object: Qualified / Rejected / No-show / Declined / Rescheduled.
🔄 Qualified = move opp to Value Alignment. Rejected = close the opp. No-show/Declined = XDR reschedules.
⚠️ Keep your calendar updated — Chilipiper routes to your live availability in real time.
