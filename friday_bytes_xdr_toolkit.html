How to work a cadence

1️⃣ Enrol prospects — confirm the right cadence for their stage first.
2️⃣ Personalise before you send — 20% personalisation = 2× reply rate.
3️⃣ Multi-channel it — red steps auto-fire, green = your action. Email + call + LinkedIn = 77–91% more replies.
4️⃣ Hot lead? Drop everything — run the 3-step Call Now cadence immediately.
5️⃣ Final "Other" step — decide: nurture, re-enrol, or remove. Never ghost a prospect.

Salesforce sync — auto vs manual

✅ Auto-syncs: emails sent, calls logged, cadence steps — as long as the SFDC record exists.
✏️ Log manually: call outcomes/notes & meeting next steps. Log before you move on.
⚠️ Gaps that break sync: no SFDC record, personal email, skipped disposition, unfilled dynamic fields.

Chilipiper New
Instant inbound lead routing & meeting scheduling. A hot lead left waiting is a dead lead — Chilipiper fixes that in under 60 seconds. ⚡

Best practices — Alina Vandenberghe, Co-CEO at Chili Piper

⚡ Speed to lead is everything. Leads contacted within 5 min are 9× more likely to convert. Chilipiper gets reps connected in under 60 sec after form submit.
🗺️ Set smart routing rules upfront. Route by territory, company size & account ownership so the right rep always gets the right lead, automatically.
📅 Let prospects self-book. Removing scheduling back-and-forth alone lifts show rates from ~60% to 80%+.
🔄 Keep your calendar availability current. Routing only works if your schedule is accurate — a blocked calendar = a missed meeting.
